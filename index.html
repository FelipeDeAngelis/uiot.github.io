What we do here?


RAISe

The unique Internet of Things open middleware for managing things, services an data. With full context-awareness support, social relationships, system visualization console and data (confiability, clustering, and more).

And it's scalable, big-data ready, open source.

DeviceOS

The micro Linux Kernel ready for the Internet of Things, that it's modularizable, hackable and ready for 80% of the microcontrollers. Working with capability of ZigBee, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Ethernet, MQTT and more.

And it's lightweight, open source.

UIMS

The ultimate user experienciable management system for the internet of things networks. With ability of network node visualization, data insights, system overview, data visualization, network management and network confiability.

And it's ux, open source.

Gateway

The Internet of Things ready router operational system that aims data distribution, data confiability and fast deliver multiple complexed data into many networks or many clients.

And it's scalable, distributed, open source.

UIoT academics

Leading the science and academics industry with advanced papers, resumés and academic content that aims for solving the most and biggest Internet of Things problems.

And it's for everyone.

UIoT intelliX

An `orange box` of algorithms, that delivers most advanced machine learning, data mining and business intelligence algorithms for managing an Internet of Things network.

And it's intelligent, capable and open source.
