Ready to Get Started?

Enjoy this UIoT quick-guides to enter on this **magic** universe!

UIoT desktop, and embedded solutions

RAISe the open source iot middleware

Ready to start to deploy an Internet of Things middleware on your House? Interested on how IoT works? Are you a small business, or a big industry leader? Don't matter! Start with RAISe, today, and experiment what IoT is in your own.

DeviceOS the open source iot device os

Want to play with your Lights? Control your Door? Or even control your Air Ventilation System? Or just play around with Arduinos? This is for you. DeviceOS transform from your Arduino to even your Computer into an IoT smart object.

UIMS the ideal solution ofr iot network management.

Ready to enjoy into a deeper look of how an IoT network works? this is for you. UIMS allows you to view alerts, insights, metrics, configurations, nodes, graphs and everything from your Iot Network.

Gateway An IoT router and distributed data deliver

UIoT gateway it's an layer-7 router, that acts as data distributor and deliver. The gateway also simplifies the send process for low-hardware devices, like Arduino Nano or Intel Galileo. Acting as low layer middleware between RAISe and the Devices. Runs perfectly on an Raspberry PI.

UIoT mobile and wearable solutions

mobileOS transform mobile into an IoT smart object.

With UIoT you can even transform your daily devices, like your phone, tablet or wearables into smart objects and integrate they into an IoT network. Mobile devices have many sensors and actors, this is great. And you can enjoy all their capabilities.
Compatible only with Android, Android War and iOS

UIMS mobile

With UIMS mobile you can get network alerts, push notifications and insights of your IoT network. You can also see which devices are offline, online, with good fidelity scores or more. This is having control of your Network in a touch.
Compatible only with Android and iOS.

UIoT solutions for industry and research

UIoT academics

Want to research and enter in the IoT community? new contributors are welcome, ever. UIoT does a lot of research and academic publications. We aim to ensure that the scientific world can get the last of IoT research. Why not read one of our papers?

Note.: you can also become an UioT researcher. Why not enter in our team?

UIoT industry and business solutions

Need something made specially for your needs? we're here to provide research and hardware solutions made specifically for you. You can explore our big database of hardware use-cases, or if you need we can deploy something for you.
